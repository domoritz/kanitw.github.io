Kanit (Ham) Wongsuphasawat.

[pronounced Guh-nit (Ham) Wohng-soo-puh-suh-wuhd]

Kanit is a PhD Student working with Prof. Jeffrey Heer at the UW Interactive Data Lab. His current research focus is on designing and building interactive systems for data analysis and data presentation. Broadly, he is interested in Human-Computer Interaction, Information Visualization and User Interface Software and Technology.

Kanit was a recipient of Fulbright Scholarship and MS in Management Science & Engineering from Stanford University, where he worked with Prof. Scott Klemmer. He also holds a B.Eng with First Honor and Gold Medal in Computer Engineering from Chulalongkorn University in Thailand. Formerly, Kanit worked at Tableau Software with Jock Mackinlay and at Google’s HCI group with Elin Pedersen.

Besides work, Kanit loves soccer, movies, books, photography, and writing. He blogs both in English and Thai.

Please feel free to look at his former projects.
