Kanit (Ham) Wongsuphasawat.

[pronounced Guh-nit (Ham) Wohng-soo-puh-suh-wuhd]

Kanit is a PhD Student working with Prof. Jeffrey Heer at the University Washington Interactive Data Lab. His current research focus is on interactive systems for data visualization and analysis. Broadly, Kanit is interested in User Interfaces, Information Visualization and Data Science.

Kanit was a recipient of Fulbright Scholarship and MS in Management Science & Engineering from Stanford University. He also holds a B.Eng with First Honor and Gold Medal in Computer Engineering from Chulalongkorn University in Thailand. Besides academic experience, he had professional experience at various data-driven technology companies including Google, Tableau Software, Thomson Reuters and Trifacta.

Besides work, Kanit loves running, soccer, movies, books, photography, and writing. He blogs both in English and Thai.
